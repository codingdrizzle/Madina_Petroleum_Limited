Who we are

About Us

The MADINA PETROLEUM Group is a service provider specializing in the transport and logistics of liquid fuels, gasoline and diesel commodities. MADINA PETROLEUM GROUP started its activities in 2006 as a public company and officially registered in Mali on August 30th, 2012.

The Group operates in the West African sub-region and directs its operational management from the head office in Mali as well as branches in Togo, Benin, Ghana and Cote d’Ivoire, Burkina Faso and Nigeria. A complete team of MADINA PETROLEUM GROUP employees is made up of first-rate specialists whose competence is the result of training companies confirmed by certificates and many years of experience in the service of the largest petrochemical plants.
